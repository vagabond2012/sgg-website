What SGG is about

We started SGG to keep South Gujarat families, students, and professionals connected across North America, with members in both the U.S. and Canada. The goal is simple: make transitions easier and growth faster through real community.

Help Newcomers

Guidance on settling in, career direction, and practical peer support for students and families.

Career Network

Resume feedback, mentoring, LinkedIn networking, referrals, and genuine one-to-one introductions.

Keep In Touch

Local and regional meetups that build social connections and professional opportunities.

Community Tenets

  • Non-profit, volunteer-led, minimal operating cost.
  • Culturally aligned and welcoming for all members.
  • No political discussion or irrelevant forwards.
  • Member information remains private and never shared with third parties.
